# TheZoneShotRL.Ver 1.0
## Project Context

**Zone Shot** is a single-player roguelite sports management game developed in Unity. It blends deep team-building strategy with the replayability, risk-reward tension, and "one more run" feeling of roguelites — all centered around basketball.

### Core Vision
You are the manager and coach of a rising basketball team. Every season is a fresh roguelite run: draft and trade players, manage training and roster synergy, upgrade your facilities through weekly events, build powerful card decks, and make critical in-match coaching decisions.

The game puts heavy emphasis on **player agency**. Every choice — from draft picks and facility investments to card usage and on-court coaching calls — can make or break your season. Fail to win the Zone Shot Championship and you restart, but you carry forward knowledge, unlocks, and legacy progression that make future runs deeper and more strategic.

### Key Gameplay Pillars
- **Roguelite Structure & Legacy**: Full seasons with high stakes. Failure teaches you, and persistent meta-progression (unlocks, legacy bonuses, knowledge) rewards smart experimentation across runs.
- **Team Management & Roster Building**: Draft, trade, and develop players with distinct playstyles(forward, Horns, and other playstyles). Optimize your squad for different strategies.
- **Facility & HQ Upgrades**: Use weekly events and resources to improve training facilities, medical care, scouting, and other support systems that directly impact your team’s performance.
- **Card-Driven Matches & Coaching**: During games, play cards to boost your team and make dynamic coaching decisions based on player skills (shooting, passing, juking/dribbling). Adrenaline mechanics and risky plays add tension and replayability.
- **Meaningful Choices & Emergent Stories**: Strong focus on cause-and-effect. A bad draft or wrong in-game call can end a promising run — but brilliant adaptation can create legendary comebacks and championship teams.

### Technical & Development Focus
- **Engine**: Unity
- **Core Systems**: Roguelite run structure, JSON save/load, card/deck system, facility progression, player archetypes & synergies, UI/UX flows, event system, and match logic.
- **Design Priorities**: Tight core loops (management → events → matches), strong player feedback, visual polish, and high replayability through meaningful choices.

The project started in mid-2024 and is being built with a clear vision of delivering a fresh take on sports roguelites — combining the strategic depth of management sims with the addictive structure of roguelites.

This context exists to keep the team (and future contributors) aligned on the "why" behind every system and feature.
